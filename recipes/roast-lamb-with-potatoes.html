Roast Lamb with Potatoes

Roast lamb with crispy potatoes

This herb roasted leg of lamb is a classic Greek dish that is wonderful for family dinner. Itâ€™s the perfect meal with the lamb roasting along with the greek style lemon potatoes all in one pan. The potatoes are tender and so flavourful as they cook along with the lamb and lamb drippings.

Ingredients

  • 1kg Roast Leg of Lamb
  • Baby Potatoes
  • Rosemary
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Remove lamb from fridge and pre-heat your oven to 220C
  2. Dress lamb olive oil, rosemary, garlic and salt
  3. Halve your potatoes and season with salt and olive oil
  4. Cook lamb for 20-mins and then reduce heat to 200C
  5. Add potatoes and cook for a further 60-mins
  6. Leave to rest for 20-mins
  7. Serve!
