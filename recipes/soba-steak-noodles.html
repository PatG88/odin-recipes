Soba Steak Noodles

Quick and easy soba steak noodles

A seriously tasty bowl of noodle goodness! These Beef Soba Noodles are dressed with a fantastic dressing reminiscent of traditional Japanese soba sauce. Quick to make, youâ€™ll have this on the table in just 15 minutes. Fantastic for hot summer nights or a midweek dinner option, and a great way to make a little bit of steak stretch further!

Ingredients

  • Sobe Cup Noodles
  • Mixed Stir Fry Vegetables
  • Rump Steak
  • Sesame Oil

Steps

  1. Pre-heat your wok
  2. Add the vegetables and fry for 5-mins
  3. Remove vegetables and fry your steak to your preference
  4. Leave steak to rest
  5. Add water to your cup noodles and rest for 3-mins
  6. Remove water from noodles
  7. Cut steak into small slices and mix all ingreadients into the wok
  8. Add soba sauce and fry for 3-mins
  9. Serve!
